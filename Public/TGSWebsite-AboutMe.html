Hi everyone! My name is Melissa Geoghegan.

I've been crocheting since I was about 8 years old. I gained an interest in crocheting after seeing my grandmother crochet and knit. I has always been fasicnating to me how we can turn yarn into things like clothes, accesories, plushies, and so many other possibilites. I went on to looking up videos and tutorials online to learn more. I would look up tutorials and them have to look up another tutorial to learn how to do a certain stitch.

The amount of different stitches is yet another thing I love about crocheting.
